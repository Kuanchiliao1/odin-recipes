Beef Noodle Soup

Description

This is flavorful noodle soup born in Taiwan. It's a very difficult recipe, so I'll skip a couple(most) of the steps, but you should be fine. Maybe. Anyway it tastes amazing! The flavors are complex and rich, and the beef almost melts in the mouth. One of my fav foods ever!

    Ingredients:
  • Beef
  • Noodle
  • Bowl of soup
    Steps:
  1. Make the beef noodles
  2. Get out your bowl of soup
  3. Add the beef noodles to the bowl
  4. Done!
