Yogurt Soup

Description

I'll be real, I had no idea this existed. I was bored one day and thought dang what if someone made a yogurt soup?? Well, I looked it up and here it is! Enjoy!

    Ingredients:
  • Bowl
  • Yogurt
  • Soup
    Steps:
  1. Take out the bowl
  2. Pour soup in the bowl
  3. Mix yogurt with soup
  4. Done!
